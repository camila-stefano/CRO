Payroll deductions: Your employer may give you an option to fund your HSA yourself through payroll deductions, one-time or recurring.
  • Pre-tax contributions
  • Money goes to the HSA before ANY taxes are paid
Individual contributions: You can also open and fund an account on your own, which is helpful if you are self-employed or your employer does not offer an HSA. You can set up recurring contributions monthly or at a cadence that works best for you as a predictable and manageable way to fund your account, or you can make one-time contributions.
  • Tax-deductible contributions (if outside your employer)
Contributions by others: Other people can make contributions to your HSA on your behalf, and it is tax-free.
  • Tax-deductible contributions
